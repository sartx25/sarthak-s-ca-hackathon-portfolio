About Me

Hello,I’m Alexandra Rosie—a passionate photographer and videographer dedicated to capturing the moments that matter most. My journey began with a simple camera and a love for storytelling, and over the years, I’ve turned that passion into a fulfilling career. For me, every click of the shutter is an opportunity to turn fleeting moments into lasting memories.


With over 5 years of professional experience, I specialize in a variety of photography and videography styles, including weddings, portraits, lifestyle shoots, and creative branding projects. My goal is to bring your unique vision to life—whether it’s preserving the joy of a special occasion or crafting stunning visuals for your personal or professional brand.


Beyond the lens, I’m a firm believer in building connections and creating experiences that go beyond just taking pictures. I aim to make every client feel comfortable, confident, and celebrated in front of the camera. Let’s work together to create something extraordinary that you’ll cherish forever.
